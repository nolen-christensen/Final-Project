Handicap Calculator

Your Handicap Index is a measure of your potential ability. It allows golfers of different skill levels to compete fairly by adjusting their gross score.

The World Handicap System (WHS) formula: Handicap Differential = (Adjusted Gross Score − Course Rating) × 113 / Slope Rating

Your index is the average of the best 8 differentials from your last 20 rounds, multiplied by 0.96.
